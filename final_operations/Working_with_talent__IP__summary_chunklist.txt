**IMPRINT PROJECTS CUERVO**

**Philosophy Approach**

Our activations rely on talent. We need talent to believe in what we're doing.

=== SEMANTIC SUMMARY CHUNK 14/14 ===
Talent that 'buys in' to a project vision yields better outcomes for clients. **What do we mean by 'talent'?**

Talent ecosystem: artists, creators, managers, booking agents, PR reps, tour managers, and combinations of these. **How we approach talent**

* Start with credibility: knowledgeable and enthusiastic
* Collaborative: value talent's perspective and ideas
* Make participation easy: clear, inspiring, and achievable giveget

**Best Practices**

* Be open and experimental, willing to pivot
* Offer "tiers" in the collaborative invitation
* Allow talent's expertise to inform the program
* Approach with empathy: mindful of bandwidth and comms preferences
* Simplicity is key: bundle asks, avoid overwhelming talent

**Process**

* **Pre-Production**
 + PHASE 5:
 - Brief artist (in person or on a call)
 - Ideate location, develop projects or content with artist
 - Secure tools, resources, materials, space, etc. - Coordinate dates, times for shoots or projects
 - Create and share call sheets, load-in and load-out plans
 - Secure rider or other asks
* **Production**
 + PHASE 6:
 - Greet talent on site, if applicable
 - Be available for questions, guidance
 - Oversee creative direction
 - Manage execution
 - Initiate progress check-ins
 - Develop promotional toolkit
* **Promotion**
 + PHASE 7:
 - Inform talent of go-live dates
 - Route assets materials for artists' own channels
 - Draft suggested post copy, talking points, or tags
 - Oversee posting and track reactions, engagement, performance
 - Negotiate promotional alternatives, as needed
* **Post-Production**
 + PHASE 8:
 - Request materials from artist
 - Review and feed back to artist, collating input from client IP
 - Approve final submissions
 - Share photos, assets, or other ephemera from project for artist
 - Send thank you to artist
 - Intro artist to client for future opportunities, if applicable
 - Survey talent and ask for feedback on experience

**Tools**

* Talent tracker: track outreach, communication with artists, and status of artist deliverables
* Onesheet: provide overview of program to prospective talent
* Creative Brief: detail guidelines and parameters for artist
* Talent Toolkit: collate assets, info, and copy for promoting project
* Contract: formalize artist's participation and outline asks

**RASCI**

* High-level, which teams are responsible for what
* Timeline flow visualization
* Establish expectations for what happens, and recommended timelines

**Our Role**

* Mediators between talent and client
* Help talent understand the process of working with brands
* Advocates for talent: consider their bandwidth and best interests
