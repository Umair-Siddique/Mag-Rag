**Imprint Projects Cuervo**

**Roles**

* **Head of Strategy**: Magali Charmot
* **Lead Strategists**: senior strategists with background in brand strategy and partnerships, content creative strategy
* **Comms Strategists**: have backgrounds in social strategy and comms, responsible for research, comms ecosystems, and rollouts
* **Programmers**: lead program plans, develop themes, topics, and curate talent recommendations
* **Community Managers**: manage day-to-day activity on client channels, support community health and engagement

**Strategy Tools**

* **Overview**: frameworks and templates for common understanding of strategy at Imprint
* **Creative Strategy Tools**: Reference Library, Ideation Process Brief Templates, Platform Strategy Template, Comms Framework Rollout Templates, Asset Templates
* **Ambassador Strategy Template**: Ambassador Toolkit Template
* **Workshop Templates**: Talent Management Process Toolkits, Structural Engineering Framework Tools Templates

**Team Workflow**

* **Strategist Updates**: manager director weekly on strategy
* **Weekly Check-ins**: one-off check-ins to address specific needs
* **Meeting Purpose**: track project health, discuss team workflow, address questions

**Project Lifecycle**

* **Kick-off**: internal team kick-off, strategist adds new project board to team Asana
* **Plan**: strategist adds key deliverable dates, working sessions to Asana
* **Round 1**: strategist works through R1, sends to Director, 1hr working session
* **Round 2**: strategist works through R2, sends to Director, 1hr working session
* **Round 3**: strategist works through R3, sends to Director, 1hr working session
* **Recap**: strategist develops project recap with support from Director

**Ways of Working**

* **Concept**: kick-off with client
* **Brief**: cultural audit, ideation, comms plan, rollout, asset plan
* **Executional Briefs**: creative production, feedback, launch, recap, measure

**Collaboration**

* **Strategists collaborate with other teams**: throughout project lifecycle
* **Check-ins**: around each round, strategist with support from Director brings project from 0-1
